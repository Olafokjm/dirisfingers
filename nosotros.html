About Us

We are a company dedicated to the marketing, distribution and sale of cheese fingers with high quality standards, which seeks to satisfy the needs of customers and comply with their policies effectively and efficiently.

Our Roots

D´iris Deditos was founded more than 25 years ago in the kitchen of a family home from the city of Cartagena de Indias. In its beginnings, it sold products such as egg arepas, caramañolas, empanadas, fritters, which were very successful. We created this new line of innovative products in the gastronomy of snacks to be able to reach other neighborhoods and points of sale.

Committed to the production process of semi-finished foodstuffs which go through an operation and transformation process to later be frozen and finally sold to the public.
